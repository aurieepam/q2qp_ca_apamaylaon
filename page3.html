ABOUT US

At AurieCare, we are passionate advocates for our four-legged friends â€“ dogs! Founded in 2017, our organization is driven by a group of dedicated individuals who share a common goal: to be the voice for those who cannot speak for themselves. Our advocacy spans a wide range of issues affecting dogs, including animal cruelty, neglect, abandonment, and the promotion of responsible pet ownership. What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to making a tangible impact in the lives of dogs. Whether it's through educational initiatives, community outreach programs, or direct intervention in cases of abuse, we strive to create positive change for dogs everywhere.

MISSION

Our mission is to be the unwavering voice for dogs, advocating for their rights and promoting responsible pet ownership. We strive to create a compassionate and informed community that actively works towards the betterment of dogs' lives.

VISION

A world where every dog lives a life of love, respect, and well-being, free from cruelty and neglect. We envision a society that recognizes and values the unique bond between humans and dogs, ensuring their rights, welfare, and happiness.
