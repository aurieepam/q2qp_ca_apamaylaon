Our Background

AurieCare is not just an organization, it's a community-driven movement fueled by the collective belief that every act of kindness, no matter how small, can create a ripple effect of positive change. Together, we advocate for the voiceless, raise awareness about the plight of stray dogs, and inspire others to join our cause.

PAWCHIEVEMENTS

Transformed the Lives of 5k Furry Friends

In the past six years, our advocacy has made a significant impact on the lives of over 5,000 stray dogs all over Davao City, providing them with shelter, care, and a chance for a brighter future.

Forged Partnerships with 58 Animal Welfare Allies

Collaborating with 58 organizations, our advocacy has strengthened the collective effort to improve the well-being of stray dogs across the nation.

Distributed P16 Million in Donations and Aid

As of December 2023, our advocacy has garnered an impressive P16 million in donations from compassionate individuals and corporations. Additionally, we've provided P5 million in relief assistance for strays affected by various crises.

Emergency Response Heroes

In times of crises, our team swiftly responded, offering P38 million in aid during the aftermath of natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and wildfires that impacted stray dog communities.

Educating 150 Communities on Responsible Pet Ownership

Through our comprehensive outreach program, we've educated 150 communities on responsible pet ownership, empowering individuals to become compassionate caregivers for their furry companions.
