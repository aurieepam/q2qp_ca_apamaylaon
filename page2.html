INITIATIVES

Operation Snuggle Squad

A heartwarming initiative where volunteers spend dedicated time each week snuggling and providing comfort to stray dogs in shelters, promoting socialization and trust-building.

Operation Waggin' Wheels

A mobile adoption unit that travels to different neighborhoods, bringing adorable stray dogs directly to potential adopters. Aims to make the adoption process more accessible and convenient.

INVOLVEMENTS

PAWS (Philippine Animal Welfare Society) and AurieCare have formed a dynamic partnership since 2018 to champion the welfare and rights of dogs across the Philippines. This enduring collaboration has been dedicated to various initiatives aimed at improving the lives of dogs, promoting responsible pet ownership, and addressing pressing issues within the canine community.

Since 2010, the partnership between the Four Paws International and AurieCare has been a driving force in advancing the well-being of dogs throughout the Philippines. Together, these organizations have dedicated their efforts to a range of initiatives aimed at improving the lives of dogs, advocating for their rights, and addressing critical issues within the canine community.
